## What is Yield Farming in Simple Terms

**Yield Farming** is like a bank deposit in the DeFi world. Tokens are provided to a protocol's liquidity pool, and the protocol pays interest in return.

**Key differences from a bank:**
- No intermediary — only a smart contract
- Higher interest rates (5-100% vs 5-10% in banking)
- Higher risks (smart contracts, impermanent loss)

### How Yield Farming Works

**Basic scheme:**

1. **User** locks tokens in a liquidity pool (e.g. ETH/USDC)
2. **Traders** use the pool for token swaps
3. **Fees** from swaps are distributed among liquidity providers
4. **Additionally** the protocol may issue governance tokens (UNI, AAVE, CRV)

**Example:**

$10,000 is provided to an ETH/USDC pool on Uniswap V3.
- Weekly fees: $150
- UNI token rewards: $50
- **Total per week:** $200 (APY ≈ 104%)

## Impermanent Loss

**What it is:**

Impermanent losses occur when token prices in a pool change relative to each other. The result is less money than simply holding the tokens.

**Formula:**

```
IL = (2 × √(new_price / old_price)) / (1 + (new_price / old_price)) - 1
```

**Example:**

Liquidity provided to ETH/USDC pool:
- ETH: $2,000
- USDC: $2,000
- **Total:** $4,000

One month later:
- ETH: $4,000 (+100%)
- USDC: $2,000 (unchanged)

**Result:**
- If held: $6,000
- In pool: $5,656
- **Impermanent loss:** $344 (5.7%)

**How to protect:**
1. Choose stable pairs (stablecoins)
2. Use hedging (short futures)
3. Monitor positions and rebalance

## Yield Farming Risks

### 1. Smart Contract Hacks

**Examples:**
- Poly Network (2021): $611M stolen
- Wormhole (2022): $320M stolen
- Euler Finance (2023): $200M stolen

**How to protect:**
- Choose protocols with audits (Certik, Trail of Bits)
- Check TVL and uptime
- Don't invest more than willing to lose

### 2. Rug Pull (Fraud)

**Examples:**
- Squid Game Token (2021): $3.3M stolen
- AnubisDAO (2021): $60M stolen

**How to protect:**
- Check team (public or anonymous)
- Review token distribution
- Avoid projects promising 1000% APY

### 3. Stablecoin Devaluation

**Example:**
- UST (Terra) in May 2022: dropped from $1 to $0.01
- Investors lost $40+ billion

**How to protect:**
- Diversify stablecoins (USDC, USDT, DAI)
- Avoid algorithmic stablecoins
- Monitor issuer reserves

### 4. Liquidation (for lending)

**Example:**
- Borrowed $10,000 with ETH collateral
- ETH dropped 30%
- Position liquidated with 5-10% penalty

**How to protect:**
- Keep LTV (Loan-to-Value) < 50%
- Monitor position
- Have reserves for additional collateral

## Yield Calculator

**APY calculation formula:**

```
APY = (1 + APR / n)^n - 1

where:
- APR — annual rate without compound interest
- n — number of compoundings per year
```

**Example:**

- APR: 50%
- Compounding: daily (n = 365)

```
APY = (1 + 0.50 / 365)^365 - 1 = 64.8%
```

## FAQ

**How much is needed to start?**

From $100-500. But gas fees on Ethereum can be high ($10-50 per transaction). For smaller amounts, use L2 (Arbitrum, Polygon).

**Is it possible to lose everything?**

Yes. Risks: smart contract hack, rug pull, token devaluation. Don't invest more than willing to lose.

**What is auto-compounding?**

Automatic reinvestment of rewards to increase yield (compound interest). Services: Beefy Finance, AutoShark.

**Do taxes need to be paid?**

Yes. In most countries, farming is taxed as income (at the moment of receipt) + capital gains tax (on sale).

**Can tokens be withdrawn at any time?**

Yes. Unlike a bank, there is no lock-up. But high volatility can cause impermanent losses.
